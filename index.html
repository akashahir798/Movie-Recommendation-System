🔧 How It Works

📚 Content-Based Filtering

Uses movie features (genres, keywords, tags) with TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity to find movies similar to what you liked.

👥 Collaborative Filtering

Uses user-movie rating matrix with item-based similarity and mean normalization to predict ratings based on similar users' preferences.

🎯 Hybrid Approach

Combines both methods with weighted scoring: 25% Content + 75% Collaborative
